Do not wake up the bear

[Add some information about your project here. You can make this more attractive by changing the css and adding some images if you like.]

You have found yourself in a cave next to a sleeping bear. Your goal is to escape the cave without waking up or disturbing the bear too much. In order to escape you will need to perform a task or puzzle while staying as silent as possible. The task or puzzle is designed to require collaboration with the twist that the communication needs to be done silently. The sounds you emit are visualised in the cave, and if you make too much noise, the bear will wake up and you will lose the game. If you complete the tasks without waking up the bear, your score is decided upon how much cumulative noise you make.
