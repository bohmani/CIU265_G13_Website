Design goal

We want to explore methods of communication other than verbal communication. By designing the puzzle in a way that requires bodily movement as a medium for communication and careful interpersonal physical synergy to complete the puzzle, we hope to explore the topic of teamwork in a novel way.

The Puzzle

The puzzle will be a sort of carnival game where two players are balancing a board with two ropes attached to a pulley system. On the wall behind the board, there are several holes with animals next to them. The goal is to balance a rock-looking ball to the correct hole through cooperation.

The Room

Another player must traverse a tunnel into a room that will display one of the animals on the screen. The two rooms are connected by a window so the player that sees the character must charade to the others to convey which hole is the correct one. After the players have delivered the ball to the correct hole a new character will be displayed and the process must be repeated. This will either continue until they have completed a set amount or the goal is to complete as many rounds as possible within a set time limit.
